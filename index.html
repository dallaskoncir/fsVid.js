fsVid.js

A simple jQuery plugin for full screen background videos with automatically generated poster images.

What is fsVid.js?

fsVid.js is a super lightweight jQuery plugin that will allow you to add a full screen video background to your site easily. Alright, great but why should I use this instead of any of the other plugins out there? On top of the fact that it is lightweight and responsive, I added a little trick that will take the first frame of your video (or any other frame you want) and use that as the poster image. No screenshots! No Extra set up!

How to use fsVid.js

1

Grab the project from Github.

2

If you haven't already, grab a copy of jQuery and link that in your html document. Otherwise skip to step 3.

3

Link popcorn.js, popcorn.capture.js and fsVid.min.js in your html document. Be sure to load them after jQuery and in that order.

4

Link to the fsVid.css file in the head of your html document.

5

Add your html markup, here is an example:

<section class="video">
<video>
<source src="your-video.webm" type="video/webm">
<source src="your-video.mp4" type="video/mp4">
</video>
</section>
